Badminton Gear

Rackets

A specialized, lightweight, and durable tool designed for striking a shuttlecock across a net.


Shuttlecock

An object with an open conical shape designed to be hit across the court. Usually made of feathers, plastic, or nylon.


Shoes

A specialized indoor court footwear designed for quick, explosive, and repetitive movements on the court.
